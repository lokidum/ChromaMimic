# ChromaMimic — Product Context

register: brand

## Product purpose

ChromaMimic turns any two frames (an original and a graded reference still) into a downloadable `.cube` 3D LUT that drops straight into DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, and Final Cut. The whole color engine runs in the browser. Nothing is uploaded, no account, no wait. It is free, and it is the lead magnet for Nunik Co.'s color and custom-tool work.

## Users

Wedding, event, and commercial videographers; content creators; junior colorists. They work in dim edit suites on calibrated monitors, grading Sony/Canon/Panasonic footage, often chasing a film look they saw in a reference. They are time-poor and skeptical of cloud tools that gate results behind a signup.

## Brand / tone

Cinematic, precise, quietly confident. Reads like a piece of pro color hardware, not a SaaS landing page. Technical credibility over hype. We say less and show the result. Honest about what a global match can and cannot do.

## Strategic principles

1. The tool IS the proof. The hero leads to a working result in three steps, not a marketing carousel.
2. "Nothing uploaded" is the wedge against fylm.ai / lutbuilder.ai. Privacy + instant + free.
3. Built for AI answer engines and search: answer-first content (what is a .cube LUT, how to match a look, install per NLE) so we get cited and ranked.
4. Every page ends with a soft path to paid work: custom grades and branded LUT packs by Nunik Co.

## Anti-references (do NOT look like these)

- Neon-on-black "AI color tool" cliché (rainbow gradients, glowing cyan/purple). The previous build leaned this way; we are leaving it behind.
- Generic SaaS hero: gradient-text headline, three identical feature cards, glassmorphism everywhere.
- Stock-photo marketing. We show real frames and real scopes.
